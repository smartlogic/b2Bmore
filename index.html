SmartLogic

SmartLogic is a custom web and mobile software development company in Baltimore. Our clients range from startups to Stanley Black & Decker.

Downtown Partnership of Baltimore

Downtown Partnership of Baltimore is an independent nonprofit organization working with businesses and property owners, residents, employees, and visitors in the core of Baltimore City. The Partnership hosts networking events for the business community, including the Annual Meeting each fall, and produces independent research and reports such as the State of Downtown Report, the Economic Impact study, and the annual Development Report.
